SECTION 4
Confronting R5
Is Crypto Unpredictable?
📜R5 (Sigaki et al., 2025): BTC/ETH permutation entropy Hnorm ≈ 0.985–0.987 at d=5 on hourly data. Near-Brownian noise.
💡Our counter: R5 uses time bars (unconditional). Dollar bars use information-driven sampling — if Hnorm is lower, structure exists that time bars miss.
🎯Conditional vs unconditional: Even Hnorm = 0.97 means 3% ordinal complexity is present. Combined with Neff ≈ 3,000, a 2–3 pp DA edge IS detectable.
🚫Honest resolution: If crypto IS near-Brownian, the recommender's value is knowing when NOT to trade. This is still a valid thesis contribution.
